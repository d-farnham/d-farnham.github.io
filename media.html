Speaker Bio

David Farnham is a seasoned climate scientist and data strategist, renowned for his work in translating complex climate data into actionable insights for diverse sectors. As Senior Director of Data Science at ClimateAi, he leads efforts to develop next-generation forecasting tools for agricultural, energy, and financial applications. With a background spanning research at Stanford and Columbia University, David brings both academic rigor and practical experience to his presentations.
