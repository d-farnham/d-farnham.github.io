About Me

Bridging Climate Science & Decision-Making

As the Senior Director of Data Science at ClimateAi, I lead a dynamic team of 11 data scientists, physical scientists, and ML engineers focused on developing AI-driven climate forecasting tools. My work centers on translating complex climate and weather systems into actionable insights for agriculture, finance, and infrastructure resilience.

With a background spanning postdoctoral research at Stanford's Carnegie Institution for Science and a PhD from Columbia University, I've dedicated my career to enhancing resilience in water and energy infrastructure through innovative data approaches.

Core Values

Clarity Through Complexity

I believe in making complex climate science accessible and actionable. By translating advanced research into practical tools, I help organizations navigate climate uncertainties with confidence.

Integrity in Modeling Uncertainty

Climate science involves inherent uncertainties. I'm committed to transparent communication of confidence levels and limitations, ensuring users can make informed decisions.

Designing for Actionable Decision-Making

The ultimate goal of my work is to enable better decisions. I focus on creating tools and insights that directly support strategic planning and operational improvements in the face of climate challenges.
